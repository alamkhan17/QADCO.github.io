Lockers Services

Installation :

Before installation of tiles the ceiling grid shall be checked for line, level and square and all electrical and AC fittings shall be completely installed

Openings in tiles for the ceiling fittings supported by the tiles shall be cut with care to achieve clean edges to the cut.

Where the cut tiles are required at walls and bulkhead, full length cuts shall be made with a sharp knife through the face and back into the gypsum core so that the tile can be trimmed to a clean line.

After installation the ceiling shall be rechecked for the level and adjusted where necessary.
