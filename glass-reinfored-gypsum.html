QADCO Gypsum Ceiling materials are not only non-combustible, but also less likely to emit toxic gas or smoke, or to melt, break, or become deformed under normal fire conditions

Effective thermal Insulation

QADCO Gypsum products offer high Standard of thermal conductivity and resistance. Due to this superior thermal insulation characteristic room comfort is maintained at all times, greatly saving in cooling and heating expenses.

Superior Sound Absorption

QADCO Ceiling tiles have superior sound absorption and insulation characteristics, creating a comfortable quiet room environment.

All QADCO Gypsum product are asbestos free. Use of asbestos that can have adverse effects on the human body.
