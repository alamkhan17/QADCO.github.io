Product are made according to ASTM, JIS, CHS relative articles, These matrial also approved accordingly. Owing to looks like a revised T It is usually called T Bar. These product are made by hot Dip galvanized steel with different cap material. Our T bar can match the relative article of ASTM, JIS & CNS also pay attention to the fire resistance condition.
